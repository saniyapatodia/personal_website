about me

hi, i'm saniya. 22, studying econ and science at unsw, building things, learning fast, and trying to create a life i'll actually be proud of.

my story

raised in kolkata (india), based in sydney (australia). studying economics (data) and science (maths) at unsw. i love travelling, and meeting people from completely different walks of life. i can spend a morning buried in a dataset and genuinely look forward to what’s happening that evening. that balance isn’t accidental, i work at it.

what drives me

i’ve dealt with autoimmune conditions that knocked me around for a while. my dad has battled cancer. so when i think about using data and technology to fix healthcare in india, it’s not a pitch, it’s personal. i’m on the other side of it now and that makes me want to build something that actually matters.

how i work

i sit at the intersection of data and product. i like understanding why something isn’t working, finding it in the numbers, and then actually doing something about it. i build to think, not just to ship. currently looking for product and data roles where that combination is useful.
